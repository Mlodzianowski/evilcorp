The EvilCorp Red Team Training Environment



1.) Red Team

True Red Team assessments are different from traditional pen testing. They follow some pen testing methodologies, but are more targeted and focused — designed to test the organization’s detection and response capabilities.


2.) Hacking Web Applications

This training walks you through different exercises related to hacking modern web applications. You will also receive tons of materials and additional exercises that you can complete in your own. You can take advantage of VMs and Docker containers that were created to practice your skills. 

3.) EvilCorp CTF

90 minute Capture the Flag, application validation style, score points for exploiting WebApp Weaknesses
